Learn Common Hindi Verbs the Fun Way!

These verbs are like action superheroes. Read each card and play the mini game to remember them.

खाना (khānā)
Pronunciation: kah-naa
Meaning: to eat
Fun Challenge: Draw your favorite food and say “मैं खाना खाता हूँ” (I eat food).
पीना (pīnā)
Pronunciation: pee-naa
Meaning: to drink
Fun Challenge: Pretend to drink water and shout “मैं पानी पी रहा हूँ!” (I am drinking water).
चलना (calnā)
Pronunciation: chal-naa
Meaning: to walk
Fun Challenge: Walk like a robot around the room and say “हम चलते हैं” (We walk).
देखना (dekhna)
Pronunciation: dek-hnaa
Meaning: to see/watch
Fun Challenge: Point at something and say “मैं इसे देखता हूँ” (I see it).
लिखना (likhna)
Pronunciation: likh-naa
Meaning: to write
Fun Challenge: Write your name in Hindi and say “मैं लिखता हूँ” (I write).
Tip: Use the verbs in short sentences: "मैं खाना खाता हूँ", "वह पानी पीती है".
